Overview

My engagement with Holiday Inn Club Vacations (HICV) focused on leading a crucial digital transformation initiative. I defined the product strategy for consolidating a fragmented ecosystem into a unified digital experience, balancing feature delivery (such as open-dated vacation packages and timeshare marketing) with the architectural vision for a modern, scalable platform. Furthermore, I successfully implemented an agile process shift, moving integration 'left' to mitigate risks, ultimately enabling HICV to become the first timeshare company to allow 100% of the reservation process to be done online.

Key Accomplishments

Transformation & Strategy:

Led the product strategy for consolidating a fragmented ecosystem into a unified digital experience. Conducted comprehensive discovery phases to align internal stakeholders across marketing, sales, and corporate interests.

Roadmapping & Lifecycle Management:

Defined and managed the State of the Product roadmap. Successfully balanced immediate feature needs—such as open-dated vacation packages and timeshare marketing—with the long-term vision of a modern, scalable architecture.

Vendor & Technical Alignment:

Bridged the gap between business objectives and technical execution. Guided engineering and architecture teams through critical build-vs-buy decisions, including headless CMS selection, MuleSoft API integrations, and third-party identity (Okta) and payment (Freedom Pay) setups.

Agile Delivery & Risk Mitigation:

Structured iterative project delivery by defining staggered sprints, mapping functional slices, and overseeing user testing. Proactively identified integration risks and mitigated blockers across legacy master data management (MDM) systems to ensure on-time feature releases.
