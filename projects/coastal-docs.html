I worked with a local business to take their existing brand and give it an online presense. I developed and designed their site using a barebones Wordpress theme that I could make our own. Coastal Docs LLC is now a host to their own website and I walked them through setting up their hosting site with thorough documentation so they can make future changes. I created their Google business page so they would have a presence on Google Maps and in Google search results. I set up their Google search console and uploaded sitemaps and made sure Google was crawling correctly, etc.
