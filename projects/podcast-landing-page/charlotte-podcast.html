I Was a Teenage Movie Critic
Apple Podcasts Soundcloud Stitcher

From 1997-2002, then-teenagers Joanne Spataro and Griffin Van Malssen reviewed movies for the Observer’s KidFlix/Teen Screens column. Now they're diving back into those old films to see how their opinions have changed, and reminisce about the best kind of misspent youth!
