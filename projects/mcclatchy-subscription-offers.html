mcclatchy subscription offers pages

2019 | ui, ux, prototyping view mobile prototypeview desktop prototype

One of my long-term projects with McClatchy is our subscription platform. This complicated product has many different moving parts that all mesh together to connect our subscriptions across all devices and sites. All 30 McClatchy markets use this, and we offer different subscription packages, offers, and ways to subscribe. This project takes a lot of UX research, user studies, and planning to achieve our goals.

Our new subscription offers page is the second step after our new paywalls. Using the same card-based system and guidelines from our Saratoga design system, I created a new landing page for our users looking to learn more about all of our subscription offers.
