Overview

As Lead Product Consultant for Dow Jones and The Wall Street Journal, I drove product strategy and delivery across a high-stakes portfolio of five concurrent iOS initiatives. My work included directing a full mobile modernization effort across three major consumer brands and leveraging architecture-informed decisions to accelerate the delivery of key features like the native print-replica reader. A crucial part of my role was orchestrating a strategic handoff of the Market Data product to the client's internal team, focusing on operational continuity and maintainable documentation.

Key Accomplishments

Multi-Workstream Portfolio Leadership at a Major Financial News Publisher

Owned product strategy and delivery for five concurrent iOS workstreams inside one of the world's most-read financial news apps: Market Data redesign, Notifications Inbox, the WSJ Print Edition, Polymarket integration, and Search redesign, all from a cross-program delivery role. Carried each from discovery and scoping through sprint execution and launch readiness, while serving as the named product owner on weekly executive status.

Mobile Modernization Across a Three-App Consumer Ecosystem

Directed an 8-month redesign of the financial data experience inside the WSJ, MarketWatch, and Dow Jones iOS apps; three distinct consumer brands running on a shared codebase serving millions of subscribers. Shipped redesigned market overview, watchlist, quote-detail, and charting experiences across U.S., European, and Asian markets, plus full iPad support, all driven through a single roadmap to keep the three brands in sync.

Architecture-Informed Decisions on High-Stakes Launches

Authored the product specs and owned delivery plans for two near-term launches: a native print-replica reader that brings the WSJ's daily edition to the iPhone, and an in-app notifications feed that lets subscribers catch up on alerts they missed. On both, chose to leverage existing platform capabilities over building custom backend services: compressing timelines, removing integration risk, and getting the team to beta in weeks rather than quarters.

Strategic Handoff & Operational Continuity

Orchestrated the transition of a flagship product line from an external consulting pod to the client's permanent in-house team, including engineering ramp-up, structured knowledge transfer, and architectural documentation. Designed the handoff so the platform stays maintainable long after the consulting engagement ends, a deliverable I treat as seriously as any feature ship.
