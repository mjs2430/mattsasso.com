Privacy Policy — Sports Scores Live Activity

Effective date: 8 May 2026

Summary

Sports Scores Live Activity does not collect, store, or transmit personal information. The app saves your favorite team selections on your device and fetches public sports scores from an internet endpoint operated by the developer. That's it.

What's stored on your device

  • The team you've picked for each league (MLB, NBA, NHL, NFL).
  • Whether you've enabled the persistent score notification.

These values are stored in the app's private SharedPreferences and are not transmitted anywhere. Uninstalling the app removes them.

What's sent over the network

When the app refreshes scores it sends a request to a Cloud Function operated by the developer at us-central1-sports-scores-server.cloudfunctions.net. The request contains the league, the team name you've selected, and the current date. The Cloud Function fetches publicly available scoreboard data from a third-party sports data source and returns it to the app. No identifier, account, location, contact, or device information is included.

Network requests are subject to standard server logs (timestamp, IP address) retained by Google Cloud for operational purposes. These logs are not used to build a profile of you and are not sold or shared with any third party.

Optional in-app tips

The app's About screen offers an optional "tip the developer" button. If you choose to tip, the transaction is handled entirely by Google Play Billing. The developer never sees your payment details, billing address, or any other purchase information beyond the fact that a tip was received. Tips are one-time, optional, and do not unlock any features.

What we don't do

  • No analytics SDKs, no crash reporting SDKs, no advertising SDKs.
  • No account creation. No login. No email or phone collection.
  • No location access. No contacts access. No microphone or camera access.
  • No data sold or shared with third parties.

Permissions the app requests

  • Internet / network state — to fetch scores.
  • Post notifications — to display the score notification.
  • Foreground service / data sync — to keep the score notification updated while the app is closed.
  • Receive boot completed — to restore the score notification after a device reboot, if you've enabled it.

Children

The app is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect any information from them.
