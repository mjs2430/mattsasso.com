homekeeper

2017 | ui, ux, apps

Homekeeper was brought to me by a client after the branding phase and ideation of main features. I created a UX document highlighting market research and best practices for this type of app and for iOS app guidelines. I then created a user journey document use Overflow.io and presented this to stakeholders and developers. After that, I started on wireframes, then high-res mocks, then clickable prototypes. I also designed the iOS app icons based on the logo they had provided.

After design was finished, I handed off the designs and all styling guidelines to developers in the form of an "inspect" mode that lets developers see all the styles, spacing, sizing, typography, colors, etc. I continued to meet with developers via video chat during the dev cycles to answer questions and make sure we were implementing correctly.
