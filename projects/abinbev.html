My role was central to fostering enterprise alignment, including co-facilitating a massive PI Planning event for over 50 stakeholders that resulted in the client mandating all future planning sessions be held at our headquarters.

I was also trusted by executive leadership to execute strategic delivery and rescue efforts for the enterprise M360 application team, where I unblocked workflows and accelerated feature delivery. A key business impact included leading the successful 0-to-1 launch of a native survey tool, which consolidated fragmented workflows and shaved 15% off task completion time for sales and delivery employees across customer sites.
