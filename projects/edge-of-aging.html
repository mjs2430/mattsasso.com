edge of aging

2018 | ui, ux, html, js, css, wordpress visit the site

Edge of Aging’s creators run a consulting business and host a podcast aimed at industry experts. I personally met with the co-founders, and virtually met with their podcast producers so we could come up with requirements for their site.

I planned out the UX, wireframed and designed the UI in Adobe XD, and developed their site through Wordpress. The site pulls in new podcast episodes posted through their host, and has personal brand pages/blogs for the creators. Using APIs from their podcast host and custom JavaScript, I have their podcast episodes auto creating posts with text and images, as well as embedding the player in each post and on the homepage.
